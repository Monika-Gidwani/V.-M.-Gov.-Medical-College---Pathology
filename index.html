About VMPath

16Path

VMPath Faculty

The department of Pathology was established in our erstwhile private medical college at its inception in 1965. Ever since the department has metamorphosed and kept up with the ever increasing demand and technological advances of a diagnostician dominated world of medicine.

In 2026, when the 16Path website went live, the college took in 200 undergraduate and 7 postgraduate students and served the affiliated state run hospital through its departments of histopathology, cytology, haematology, blood centre, central clinical laboratory. The Department is equipped with modern equipment and machineries viz., 5 part differential cell counter, fully automated coagulator, liquid based cytology immunohistochemistry retrieval system, fully automatic tissue processor, and urine analyzer.

Our department houses a state of the art Multidisciplinary Research Unit (MRU) which focuses on non-communicable disease research with current key focus on haematopathology, especially haemoglobinopathies. Advanced equipment like Olympus Trinocular Microscope with inbuilt photoimaging capabilities, high performance liquid chromatography variant 2 and Bio-Rad-D10 haemoglobin testing system.

About 16Path

16Path

16Path Credits

16Path is a novel, web-based learning initiative by the Department of Pathology of Dr V. M. Govt. Medical College, Solapur that integrates several didactic and graphical learning techniques to enhance the training of Pathology. It aims at keeping up with the trend of self-learning in a post pandemic world whilst still ensuring the coveted teacher’s insight remains intact. Several modules like 16Path cards, live gross and microscopy demonstrations, and specimen screening protocol are one of their kind contributions by VMPath to the world of academic Pathology. Besides these, the riddles, MCQ tickers, specimen posters, research posters, lab procedure demonstrations, and patient guides are other learning aids that intend to keep the visitor engaged and informed.

  • Mentors: Dr. Sunita Dantkale, Dr. Pradeep Gadgil
  • Web Design and Development: Dr. Kaustubh Chaudhari, WIT Developer Team
  • Content Management: Dr. Jayashri Khiste, Dr. Aishwarya Gawai
