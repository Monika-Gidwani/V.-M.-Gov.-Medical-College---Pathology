Cases Credits
  • Hashimoto thyroiditis: Thyroid follicular epithelium shows oncocytic change and dense lymphocytic infiltration with germinal center.
  • Papillary thyroid carcinoma: Complex papillae lined by cuboidal cells with nuclear clearing, overlapping, crowding and intranuclear grooves, and occasion psammoma bodies.
  • Well differentiated thyroid tumor with uncertain malignant potential (low risk neoplasm): Well encapsulated, well differentiated tumor with nuclear features of papillary thyroid carcinoma. No invasion. CK19 positive.
  • Medullary thyroid carcinoma (Low grade): Subset 1: Sheets and nests of round to oval, uniform cells. Subset2: surrounding stroma shows amyloid.
